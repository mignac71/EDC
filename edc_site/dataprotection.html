Data Protection

This page outlines our approach to data protection and privacy for visitors, members, and partners of the European Dealer Council.

We collect only the data necessary to provide our services, store it securely, and never share it with third parties without explicit consent or legal obligation.

For further information or to request access to your data, please contact info@dealercouncil.eu.

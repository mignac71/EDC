Our Mission

We are the voice of the European VW/Audi dealers and service partners

Founding

Founded on March 21st, 1991, in Hanover/Germany. Open for each national Volkswagen and/or Audi Dealer Association from European countries.

Members

The association is a community of national Dealer Councils, which represent 21 European countries.

Organization

The highest organ of the association is the partner assembly, which consists of the delegates of the partner countries.

Political Body

Bodies are the presidium consisting of the president and four vice‑presidents, as well as the managing director.

Community

The association is the official representative body of the trade organizations of the Group.

Tasks

First and foremost, we see ourselves as a platform for the exchange between retailers, manufacturers, brands and national group distributors and importers.
